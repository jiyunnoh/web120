Audience Information

Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Women aged of 10-40

This could be the main target who is interested in different kinds of useful goods when reading. They prefer cool and maybe cute designs. They will probaly use mobile devices. Most of them might be students, office workers or homemakers.
  • Need to know about products and price
  • Want to know about reviews of products
  • Want subscriber's discount or special offer
  • Look for unique and cool design of products
  • Want customized products
  • Provide the product descriptions with price
  • Provide reviews content with rating below the product description
  • Notify subscription email regularly with special offers
  • Collaborate with specific brands
  • Provide contact form
  • Provide links to social media
Senior aged of 60+

This target could be our customers who feel uncomfortable when reading because of small fonts. They will probably use PC mostly and possibly mobile devices.

  • Want a simple and easy navigation
  • Look for certain products to help them reading
  • Want an easy access to the website
  • Provide a simple navigation and layout
  • Provide a special section for senior users
  • Provide a contact form
  • Provide the product descriptions with price
Readers who need recommendation

This group will be potential regular customers. They like reading and want recommendation list what to read. This website will attract them first, and then they will browse the diverse merchandise. This target will use mobile devices as well as PCs.
  • Look for recommended books
  • Want to share book reviews with other users
  • Look for unique and cool design of products
  • Want customized products
  • Provide user-customized lists of books
  • Provide a social networking page for users to share reviews
  • Provide links to social media to get wider audiences
  • Provide a contact form
Authors and publishers

This target look for some unique ways to promote their works. They might be new authors or small publishers who need promotion. They want to engrave a sentence or a short phrase from their books to merchandise. They will use both mobile devices and PCs.

  • Need to promote their works
  • Want to know reader's review and feedback
  • Want to attract more customers
  • Provide a contact form
  • Provide reader's review page or forum
  • Provide links to social media to get wider audiences
